Your data has only one owner: You.

Only you are permitted to exert control over your data. Neither MentDoc, nor any third-party can access your personal information, for any purpose/s, except those authorized by you.

We never promote or advertise our products or services to yourwalk-ins

We never connect with your patients who walk-in for treatment, nor do we advertise our products to them, in accordance with our privacy policy. Patients can choose to connect better with their respective doctors by downloading and using the MentCom Application from Google Play Store. MentDoc also does not have access to your patients’ records’ database which is stored by MentCom.

Never under any circumstances will we ever sell your data

We pursue utmost care towards the sensitivity of your personal health related data and take privacy of information very seriously. Collection, use and disclosure of information which has been designated as Personal Information or Sensitive Personal Data or Information’ under the SPI Rules requires your articulate consent, thereby ensuring complete safety of the data.
